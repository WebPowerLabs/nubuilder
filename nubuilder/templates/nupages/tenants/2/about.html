About Us

The Company

Jackson Tile and Design, LLC is a family owned business managed by Bart and Jennifer Jackson. The business was founded in October of 2001. Bart Jackson has over 30 years of industry experience installing flooring and 10 years of entrepreneurial experience managing Jackson Tile & Design. Jennifer Jackson is the Finance Manager and Chief Development Officer.

Bart’s exceptional workmanship quality created an increasing demand for his detail and installation services. So instead of working for someone else, the Jackson family decided to put his skill and expertise to better use!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best experience in customer service to all of our clients and pride ourselves on exceptional installation quality at competitive prices.
